Why It's Called March Madness

2022-03-29

My notebook and game recipe was formed off of the classic sports saying, “offense is the best defense”. I believe that the fundamental thought that constantly scoring will result in a win, will apply to March Madness. The predictors I will use are effective fg %, true shooting %, rebounds, only offensive ,three point %, free throw %, point per game true shooting % and points per possession. I added free throw % and points per possession to the recipe because I think it matters if teams can capitalize off stopping their opponent. Finally I added offensive and defensive efficiency due to team needing to stop their opponents while still scoring.

The biggest reason I believe my model did not predict correctly, other than a 15 seed to the elite 8. My model was predominately an offensive/shooting predictor and the teams that shot well all season failed to do so in the tournament.
